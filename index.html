Background-color

This refers to the color behind content on a webpage or design. It sets the overall tone or mood of the design, impacting how other elements stand out. Background colors can range from solid shades to gradients or images. It plays a key role in readability and visual appeal.

Text

Text refers to the written content displayed in a design. It conveys the main message or information to the viewer. The size, style, and alignment of the text affect how easily the message is understood. Proper contrast between text and background enhances readability.

Color

Color adds emotion and personality to a design. Different colors evoke different responses—red can signify urgency, while blue can suggest calmness. Designers use color theory to balance and harmonize color schemes for visual consistency and appeal.

Fonts

Fonts are the styles of lettering used to display text. They vary in size, weight, and form, and can be categorized as serif, sans-serif, or decorative. Fonts influence readability and tone, with simpler fonts being more legible and decorative fonts adding personality to titles.
